What is this?
This is multi-disciplinary research lab by Frédéric Cyr at the Centre for Fisheries and Ecosystem Research.
The name OceanAccess has a double meaning. It refers to both accessing the mysteries of the ocean (scientific discoveries), but also making ocean observations accessible to others (open access philosophy).
